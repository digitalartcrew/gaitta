The Story

Real Ones
Know
The Name.

Gaitta is an Oakland-bred rapper whose sound is rooted in the raw, unfiltered energy of the Bay Area. From early collaborations with regional legends to commanding full projects under his Allizm imprint, Gaitta has built a reputation on honesty, craft, and consistency.

His catalog spans over a decade and a half — from street anthems to introspective cuts — cementing his place as one of Oakland's most authentic voices. With projects like Martin Luther Lean, The Gold Standard, and The 5 Deadly Venoms EP, the music speaks for itself.
